HOW IT WORKS

PLAYERS

  • Begin with 10 CrickCoins and an 8-card deck.
  • Join battles to win experience and reputation.
  • Upgrade your cards based on activity (e.g., game participation, win/loss).
  • Build a deck or buy one suitable for your strategy and playstyle.
  • Join free tournaments and win to earn CrickCoins.
  • Rise to the top of the leaderboards and get drafted by team owners to play on their behalf.
  • Earn Gol Coins from weekly contracts; by selling cards/decks; or by sellingl upgraded cards via the Auction House.
  • Exchange Gol Coins for real money whenever you want to leave the ecosystem.
  • Withdrawal requires 10 Gol coins minimum and is only allowed once a week.

TOURNAMENTS

  • Players drafted into teams play all games exclusively on behalf of their team.
  • Tournaments always involve team vs. team play.
  • The most active teams have the highest chance of winning.
  • Scores are calculated for the team as a whole.
  • Top teams win prizes.
  • 25+ types of tournaments are available.
  • Players cannot enter into team tournaments.
  • One free tournament is available per week.

OWNERS

  • Join a Silver ($100), Gold ($1,000) or Platinum ($5,000) league.
  • Get Gol Coins, CrickCoins, and free tournament entries based on your league.
  • Track player performance and build your weekly team by offering paid contracts.
  • Enter tournaments to win Gol Coins and CrickCoins.
  • Share the spoils with your team if you wish.
  • Free and paid tournaments run all year round.
  • Tournament entry is restricted based on league.
  • Join 25+ types of tournaments with a different strategy for each.
  • Convert Gol Coins to real money when you decide to leave the ecosystem.

FAQ

What is the game about?

  1. This is a CCG like Clash Royale or Hearthstone – but centered around Cricket.
  2. You can play two roles: Player or Team Owner (with opportunities to win crypto rewards).
  3. Players collect cards, play 1v1, participate in tournaments, and rise to the top of leaderboards.
  4. Team Owners draft players and demonstrate skills as able Team Managers to win loads of real-world prizes.

How and when can I get the game?

Crypto Cricket will be released on the App Store and Google Play. Players can download the game from one of these stores when available. Team Owners can play via the mobile website. A Player WebApp is also available for players who want to win a lot of cryptocurrency rewards. The game will go live during late Q1 or early Q1 2018. There may be a beta launch a month prior to this.

Why should I become an early adapter?

Early adapters are getting hefty offers. Please contact us at cryptocricket@golive.games

How can I become a Gold or Platinum team owner?

  1. As a Team Owner you make money by doing the following:
    • Winning tournaments
    • Selling off your team to someone else
  2. What are Gold Teams? What do I get if I own a Gold Team today?
    • Gold Teams are a special league where only 128 teams are present. This number will not change. Please refer to the table for what you get when you buy a Gold Team
  3. What is a Platinum Team? What do I get if I own a Platinum Team today?
    • Platinum Teams are a special league where only 16 teams are present. This number will not change. Please refer to the table for what you get when you buy a Platinum Team
  4. As a Team Owner, can Isubstitute any team member?
    • Yes, you can substitute a team member any time. The score of the member moved out will not be included in the final team score calculation.

How do I become a player – and what are the benefits?

  1. What is the Early Bird Offer for Players?
    • Before April 1st, entry is only $1 with which you get one Gol Coins and 10 CrickCoins. The next offer is from April to June, where Players can enter at $5. The actual rate is $10, which will be effective from July 1st onwards.
  2. As a Player, how can I make money in the game?
    1. You can make money in 3 ways:
      • Create rare and legendary cards and sell each card or a deck to others
      • Getting drafted into teams. You get a weekly contract to play on behalf of a team
      • You can sell the Player itself and all its cards to someone else.
